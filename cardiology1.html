Cardiology











Our Cardiology Room

Specialists at Medica hospital provide a convenient resource for professional care close to home. Participating board-certified cardiologists diagnose and treat conditions and diseases of the heart and vascular system. Initial evaluation and diagnoses may be made here, at which time you and your physician will determine if treatment or surgery may be provided at Medica hospital. Cardiac evaluation, diagnostics and treatment offered include:

  • EKG s (resting & stress)

  • Echocardiograms

  • ABI (Ankle Brachial Index)

CARDIO-PULMONARY REHAB 1-508-957-9524
Medica hospital's outpatient cardiac rehabilitation program is designed to gradually re-condition your heart, as well as your muscles, after a heart attack, open heart surgery, angioplasty, stent or related procedure. Exercise and educational information designed to assist you in your recovery and in your heart healthy lifestyle will be provided. The program usually lasts anywhere from four to twelve weeks, although this may be modified by your needs, the recommendations of your physician, and your insurance coverage.

  • Stress testing

  • 30-day event monitoring

  • 12-lead EKG

PULMONARY REHAB

The Medica hospital outpatient pulmonary rehabilitation program is designed to effectively improve the quality of life for those with chronic obstructive pulmonary disease by providing education, testing, exercise and counseling. The curriculum intends to maximize the individual’s ability to carry out daily activities and to effectively cope with the many facets of this disease.
