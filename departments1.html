Our Departments


We are 'Medica', and We're Here to Help !











Dental Care

Dental care is provided at Medica hospital for people who require a hospital setting, such as those with disabilities, special needs and some adults.


Cardiology

Specialists at Medica hospital provide a convenient resource for professional care close to home. Participating board-certified cardiologists diagnose and treat conditions and diseases of the heart and vascular system.













Gastroenterology

Our gastroenterology services specialize in the prevention, diagnosis and treatment of diseases of the liver and digestive tract, including the stomach, duodenum, gallbladder, biliary tract, pancreas, small intestine and colon.


General Surgery

The General Surgery Department is equipped with the latest technology and infrastructure, supported by an experienced team of surgeons. The department specialises in performing all major surgeries involving abdomen, skin, breast, soft tissues, and hernia.





























Medical Lab

Our medical laboratory technologist performs complex tests that help other healthcare professionals such as physicians detect, diagnose, and treat diseases.


Neurology

Our neurology department is dedicated to promoting the highest quality patient-centered care and enhancing member career satisfaction.
