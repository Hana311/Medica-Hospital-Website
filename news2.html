January 03, 2019

Let us introduce you our new team


Emina Becirevic

On the 3 rd December 2019, our Medica hospital got new nurse Emina Becirevic. Becirevic will work in the department of neurology. She completed high medical school in Sarajevo in 2016. In 2017, Becirevic has already worked as intern in our medica hospital for 3 months where she has shown her high skills in the field of neurology. After, she performed her internship in Germany in Ludwigshafen Governmental Hospital. There, Becirevic acquired the experience for half of year. In December, 2018, Becirevic applied for nurse position in Medica hospital and accepted.







December 31, 2018

New vitamins from January, 10


Centravit

From January 10 th, you can buy new 'Centravit' vitamin capsules in all pharmacies in the city. The capsules are intended for the winter season when there is lack of D vitamin from the sun. They can be used by all, even by children from 10 years. Centravit are the capsules rich in vitamin D, vitamin C, vitamin B1, iron, minerals, niacin, pantothenic acid, magnesium. Use it for your immune system, heath skin and hair and fatigue and exhaustion reducing.
